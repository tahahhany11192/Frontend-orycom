Lesson Creation Guidelines

  • Keep videos under 15 minutes for better engagement
  • Use HD resolution (720p minimum) for video content
  • Provide concise descriptions with key learning objectives
  • Supplement with PDF materials for deeper learning
  • Test all video and PDF content before publishing
  • Add timestamps to video descriptions for key topics
  • Create quizzes to reinforce key learning points
  • Assignments should have clear deadlines and objectives
