Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our teacher media services.

What equipment do you use for shooting? +
We use professional 4K cameras, studio lighting kits, and high-quality audio equipment including lavalier and shotgun microphones. All equipment is maintained to professional standards.
Can I roll over unused hours to the next month? +
Yes! Unused hours can be rolled over for up to 3 months. This gives you flexibility during exam seasons or holidays.
What does "unlimited editing" mean in the Premium package? +
Unlimited editing means we edit whatever footage you provide, regardless of timeline length. You get 12 hours of shooting per month, and we'll edit all resulting footage at no extra cost.
Do you provide screen recording for software tutorials? +
Absolutely! We can integrate screen recordings with your camera footage, add callouts and annotations, and sync everything professionally.
How long does it take to get started? +
We can usually start within 3-5 days of signing up. The first step is a free consultation to plan your content strategy.
What is included in the Smart Board package? +
The Smart Board package includes professional interactive whiteboard setup, specialized lighting for whiteboard recording, digital annotation tools, and optimized video capture for educational content. It's perfect for math, science, and other subjects that benefit from writing and drawing explanations.
