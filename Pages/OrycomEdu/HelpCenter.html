Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a response on WhatsApp?

Our support team typically responds within 15 minutes during business hours (9AM - 6PM, Monday to Friday). Outside these hours, we'll respond as soon as possible the next business day.

What information should I provide when contacting support?

Please include your full name, student ID, course name, and a detailed description of your issue. This will help us assist you more efficiently.

Can I get help with specific course content via WhatsApp?

Yes! Our instructors can provide guidance on course materials, assignments, and concepts. For complex topics, they may schedule a video call to better assist you.

What if I need help outside business hours?

You can still message us anytime. We'll respond as soon as our support team is available. For urgent technical issues outside business hours, please check our knowledge base for immediate solutions.
